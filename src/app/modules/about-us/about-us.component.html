Kuyoro, Oluwatoyin Judy

(Head of Business Development)

Judy has a B.A in English Language and an MSc. in Organizational Behaviour both from the University of Lagos. She is a highly skilled, hands-on, goal-getting, target-oriented value-adding professional with about 13 years' experience in Investment Management, Business Development, Customer Relationship Management and Business Administration and an extensive exposure in Human Resources and Organisational Development cutting across diverse sectors of the Nigerian economy especially in the Capital Market, Financial Services and Oil and Gas where she has displayed exemplary leadership and professionalism. She has attended various related courses locally.

Lekan Olatoke

(Chief Financial Officer)

Mr. Lekan Olatoke has a Bachelor of Science in Chemistry from University of Ilorin in 2010, after which he proceeded to earn a Professional Diploma in Business Administration (PDBS) from Chartered Institute of Administration, (CIA), Nigeria in 2011, and proceeded afterwards to earned the prestigious Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree, with option in Finance in 2015,and followed it up with a Post Graduate Diploma in Economics in 2016 both from Prominent Obafemi Awolowo University. He is presently undergoing a professional masters training in Financial Engineering at World Quant University, Louisiana, USA. In addition to all these, he has, during the course of different employment, undergone several Training and Development, and currently a program candidate of Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) Institute in USA, Association of Chartered Certified Accountant (ACCA;UK),Global Associations of Risk Professionals (GARPs-FRM:USA). He is an associate member of Chartered Institute of Stockbrokers (CIS; Nigeria), Institute of strategic management of Nigeria (ISMN), and also, Association of Entrepreneurs and Technologist of Nigeria (ASETs) and a graduate member of Nigeria Institute of Management (NIM).He has over seven (7) years hands on experience as a Financial Analyst and currently works as the Chief Financial Officer at Star Capitals and Asset Management Limited, Lagos.
